About us

Ballet is a timeless art form that combines grace, strength, and storytelling through movement. With roots in the Italian Renaissance and refined in the courts of France and Russia, ballet expresses deep emotion and narrative through structured technique, elegant lines, and expressive choreography.



At Petal & Pointe, we believe that ballet is more than just a dance—it's a journey of discipline, expression, and artistry. Our studio is a welcoming space for dancers of all ages and skill levels to explore the beauty of movement in a supportive, inspiring environment.

Founded by Isabelle Laurent, a classically trained dancer with over 10 years of teaching experience, our classes combine professional technique with joyful creativity. Whether you're taking your first plié or preparing for auditions, we offer personalized guidance tailored to your unique goals.

Our mission is to nurture confidence, grace, and a lifelong love of dance. Through small class sizes, thoughtful instruction, and a focus on both artistry and discipline, we help each dancer shine—on and off the stage.

Join us, and let ballet become part of your story.




What makes us unique

Private and Group Lessons Available

We offer both one-on-one private lessons and small group classes—so whether you prefer personalized focus or a collaborative group setting, there’s something for you.

Customized Learning Paths

Every dancer is different. That’s why we tailor our instruction to meet individual goals—whether that’s building confidence, mastering technique, or preparing for auditions.

Goal-Oriented Training


From beginner basics to advanced pointe work, we help students set realistic goals and track their progress with personal feedback and support every step of the way.
